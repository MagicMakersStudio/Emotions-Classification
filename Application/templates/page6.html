Sadness is something I know very well. It can be caused by many factors. Being sad, depressed, disapointed, hopeless. Painful. The most important, is to not lose hope. Continue fighting. You won’t get anything if you do nothing but grief. People will tell you to cheer up. Just like I did. But sometimes you just can’t. So cry. Cry as much as you want, as loud as you wish. But cry. Empty your heart. Because, an empty heart is easier to heal than a heart in the middle of an icy ocean.
